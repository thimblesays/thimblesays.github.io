“Girls Just Want to Have Fun” - Cyndi Lauper

01.13.17

I am boring. After dark, I rarely venture out on social excursions. Barring me and my husband’s weekly Friday night trek down to the Urban Grill for a Hawaiian ribeye, I’m content at home.

Tonight, I met two girlfriends for dinner! It was a wonderful evening. We talked about everything and laughed a lot; it was awesome.

Enter the song by 80’s pop star Cyndi Lauper. I remember when this ditty came out. It was during the days of big hair and shoulder pads. The lyrics are far from prophetic, but they did stand for something. Friendships are important; nurturing them is essential.

Tonights blog is short. Too much fun in my day!
