“Up & Up” - Coldplay

01.10.17

The Coldplay song, "Yellow", was played on the radio while I drove to work this morning. A song that I suspect will appear in a later post. Oddly, the song "Yellow" immediately brought my attention to another one of Coldplay's songs, "Up&Up". Up&Up is a warm song.

Since I first heard this song, I found it interesting and uplifting. Tonight, I pulled up its lyrics, simply to read them and absorb their meaning. It has a slow groove but positive vibe. In the simplest terms, I think this song's message says, "Hang on...don't give up...you've got this". Some days that's all we really need and all that we should expect.

I had a yoga teacher once tell us (I'm paraphrasing here), "When someone asks you how you are, you should respond: not bad, not good". Full disclosure, at the time, I thought that it was kind of jerk thing to say. Over the course of the next day, I was kind of all right with it.

Back to Coldplay, last two lines of Up&Up are "Don't ever give up Believe in love"

Happy Tuesday!
