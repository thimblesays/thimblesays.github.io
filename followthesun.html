“Follow the Sun” - Xavier Rudd

01.08.17

StoryToday’s the beginning of the second week of January. Sundays are often reset buttons. We begin our weeks with goals, intentions, hope and vigor. In many ways, the turn of a new calendar year is a time that many people reflect on their previous year and set resolutions.

This tune is one that I love to play at the start of yoga class. It's also a song that I think about prior to rolling out my own mat. The second verse of the song goes:

Breath, breath in the air Set your intentions. Dream with care.

"Dream with care….” hmmm… that is a nugget worth noting)

I think that setting intentions is a much better idea than making resolutions. A resolution is a firm decision to do or not do something. An intention is a thing intended; an aim or plan. We could break this down further, but I imagine that you see differences.

In the referenced verse, Rudd goes on to say:

Tomorrow is a new day for everyone, Brand new moon, brand new sun.

Perhaps setting intentions each day, in our present situations will, over time, equate to something much greater.

I really hope that you check out this song. Oh, the wisdom! Have a great week my friends. Let each and everyday be filled with something that helps you grow :)
