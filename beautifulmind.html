“A Beautiful Mind” - Magnificent City Instrument

01.15.17

Today started early with the kickoff of "Body Pump 100!" I had the day’s checklist in mind.

Then it happened. I came home and thought I’d just watch one episode of "Mad Men” while I run on the treadmill.

Damnit- I’m still watching!!

Binge watching starts with that reoccurring, familiar theme song; it dances in your head like a snappy cartoon tune from your youth. That’s when the conscious decision to watch another episode starts.

Ok, one down- I’ve gotten my daily fix; I’m good.

But then there’s the lag time (approximately 20 seconds), between the end of that episode and the start of the addictive theme music inviting me to indulge in yet another helping. This is my out. No, I can’t; I’m done. But it’s like an old friend who wants to chat… I can’t, I have stuff I gotta do. Damn. Uh-oh: 5-4-3-2-1...the music's starting again and the decision’s been made: I’m on board! Now that the pressure’s off, I can sit back and enjoy the next taste.

When my husband (“Rik”) eventually stated, “You gotta stop,” today's music selection became clear.

This is the last episode for today. Please, no more theme song!! (Maybe just ONE more…).
