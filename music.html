From a very young age, I have been a lover of music. Music is rich with perspective and generates a multitude of emotions among a diverse audience. For the next year, I plan to share my curiosity and love for certain songs and artists. The songs list will happen organically. Each post will be brief and in the moment.

My posts will in no way be a reiteration of the textbook interpretation of a song, rather they will convey the personal connection I feel: why it spoke to me, what it taught me, and how it made me feel.

These songs will not be genre restricted and may hopefully dispel a story somewhere within their rhetoric.

I encourage you to check out these songs/ artists and listen for yourself.

I am so excited to start this journey into the music that shapes my thoughts and perception of the world. I encourage comments and discussion. Here we go.
