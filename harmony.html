“Harmony” - Elton John

01.13.17

Harmony was a ballad off the 1974 album Goodbye Yellow Brick Road. There is a lot of good stuff on this album. The first song on this double album is 11:07 long. I will talk about that monstrous tune at later time. It has significance ;)

Harmony was a hidden b sider that came in at a mere 2:47. I acquired this album at some point as a youngen’, but didn’t notice “Harmony” until I graduated from college. It was buried among the multiple hits of songs the album produced.

Consider the definition of the word harmony; a combination of musical notes to create chords. The second definition, however, is agreement or concord.

For this song, I have always thought of the later definition. Agreement is a hop, skip, and a jump from the word peace. Having harmony in your life looks different for everyone. It is something everyone can have.

"Harmony and me, we’re pretty good company Looking for an island In our boat upon the sea…”

Check out this sweet song. I hope that it speaks to you the way it speaks to me!
