“You Get What You Give” - The New Radicals

01.17.17

I purchased this cd in 1998-ish. The artwork was on a sunny yellow backdrop displaying a seated guy with a floppy hat on. I am uncertain why I remember the New Radicals one and only CD cover, but I do. It’s locked and loaded in my memory.

The band basically revolved around one dude. The supporting cast rotated in and out during their hyper short career. I liked song #17 the the first time I heard it on the FM radio; I searched for the mystery artist on the airwaves. Back in the day, you couldn’t simply drop a half a sentence of lyrics into google and be magically gifted a bands name. If you were lucky the DJ would chime in and extend props to the group during commercial break.

For me, all it took was one well liked canticle and my wallet made it’s way to the music store. When my kids were little, discretionary dollars were limited, but I never skimped on purchasing new music. I considered it an investment in my mental health and an enrichment tool for my children :)

"You've got the music in you Don't let go You've got the music in you One dance left This world is gonna pull through Don't give up You've got a reason to live Can't forget We only get what we give”

^^^^^ Those lyrics are motivating! Sometimes our inner self must be our cheerleader. "You get what you give" I've used those 5 words more than once!
